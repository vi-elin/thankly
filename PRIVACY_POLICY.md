# Privacy Policy for Thankly

**Last Updated: December 18, 2024**

## Introduction

Welcome to Thankly ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Thankly mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Gratitude Entries**: When you create gratitude entries in the App, this content is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, access, or transmit your gratitude entries to any servers.

### Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information**: We do not collect any device information, analytics, or usage data.

**Location Data**: We do not collect or access your location data.

## How We Use Your Information

All data you create in Thankly is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not:
- Collect your personal information
- Access your gratitude entries
- Transmit your data to our servers or third parties
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Share your information with anyone

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: All gratitude entries are stored locally on your device using secure local database storage
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your data is not synced to any cloud services
- **Device Security**: Your data security depends on your device's security measures (passcode, biometric authentication, etc.)

## Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you to write gratitude entries. These notifications:
- Are generated locally on your device
- Do not transmit any data
- Can be disabled at any time in the App settings or device settings

## Third-Party Services

Thankly does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

Thankly does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from any users.

## Data Retention and Deletion

- Your gratitude entries remain on your device until you delete them
- You can delete individual entries at any time by swiping left on any entry
- Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all your data from your device
- We do not retain any backup copies of your data

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:
- **Access**: You can access all your data within the App
- **Deletion**: You can delete any or all entries at any time
- **Export**: Currently, data export is not available, but all data remains on your device

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
